Welcome to SecureNet

Before you begin, let's understand how the scanner works.

This tool analyzes local Wi-Fi data to help you secure your digital environment.

1. Local Network Discovery

The application scans your current Wi-Fi environment to detect signal strength, channel usage, and the active encryption standard (e.g., WPA2/WPA3).

2. Machine Learning Analysis

A trained Machine Learning model evaluates your network metrics against known vulnerability patterns to generate an overall risk score (Safe, Medium, or Dangerous).

3. Expert Security Tips

Based on the findings, the AI system will provide actionable recommendations, such as updating router firmware or changing weak passwords, to harden your network security.

User Authorization Required

To proceed, you must explicitly grant permission for the application to analyze your local Wi-Fi data.
